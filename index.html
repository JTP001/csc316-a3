The odds of winning a lottery like the LOTTO MAX are incredibly low, but it's hard to really get a feel for how low your chances are just by looking at the odds.

Try to recreate a winning LOTTO MAX sequence by selecting each of your seven draws, and see how long it takes you to get a sequence that has won before.


How to Play

The below visualization shows you the most often drawn number in each of the seven draws that make up a LOTTO MAX sequence, from a dataset of all the sequences that have won before.

Clicking on a number adds it to your sequence, and then updates the visualization to show the most often drawn number in the second, third, etc draw of the sequence. Draw your seven numbers and test your luck!

Hint

The odds that you get a sequence that has won before are very low, so if you can't seem to win, try using some of the hints below. You can reveal numbers one-by-one from one of these winning sequences, or show the whole things and get a guaranteed win!
